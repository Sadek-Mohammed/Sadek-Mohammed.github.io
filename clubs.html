My school is a boarding school, and that provides ambitious students like me an opportunity to particpate in many of the school's clubs. English Club

Summary

The English Club | Writing Branch was founded by me, where I had to put a curriculum. I have designed a curriculum that consists of 10 sessions whose content was picked from the Glencoe Writer's Choice Grammar and Composition, a book recommended by my teacher. To introduce the club to the younger students, I and the other mentors have organized an orientation session that were attended by Mr. Samir Abdelrahman, english teacher at my school and the club's supervisor. Next, I have opened registeration for younger students at my school. After that, I had to distribute the 44 students over me and the other 10 mentors. For the following two months, I kept giving sessions and following the progress of the other mentors. Each session discussed a certain unit of the book, assigned a certain task, and revised the task of the previous sessions.

Deutsch Club
I started working on the Deutsch Club with the start of the 11th Grade. The main task was to mentor a class of 22 students who were new to German. That included solving practice questions, studying the curriculum, and following up via WhatsApp. In the second term, Mr. Mohammed Zaki, the school's head german teacher, wanted to improve the level of Deutsch in the school. That's why he started preparing all students in Grade 10 for the A1 exam at Goethe Institut in Kairo in October 2022. I had the duty of preparing the students to the test, alongside Abdelrahman Sameh - a classmate. The test is at the end of October, and I will update the website with statistics then.

Capstone Project Revision

Summary

One of my school's distinctive traits among the other Egyptian schools is the existence of a per-semester Capstone project that uses knowledge from all STEM subjects to solve one or more of the 11 Grand Challenges of Egypt. The capstone consists of 3 parts: Portfolio, Prototype, and Poster, where each of them requires intense revision, especially for students in the 10th Grade who are still new to that sort of academic work. After finishing the work, a mock exhibition is prepared for those students to familiarize them to the process of evaluation and presenting projects before the real exhibition. The real exhibition is presented for University Professors from the top Egyptian Universities. I have revised Capstone project through the first and second semesters of the 11th Grade, and although I do revise Capstone Projects in the 12th Grade, all statistics here belong to the 11th Grade work.

I, alongside 2 classmates, led the mock exhibition and discussion of 5 groups. Discussing a project includes asking test questions, giving constructive feedback, and delivering an evaluation to the Capstone supervisor.
