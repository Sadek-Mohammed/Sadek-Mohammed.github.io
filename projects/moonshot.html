Moonshot #beapriate Challenge
This challenge is a competition for creative minds, ages between 14 and 19. The 2021 challenge, in which my team and I participated, contained about 800 young changemakers from 80+ countries, forming 65 teams. My team and I got the 2 nd place for an idea that helps people with deaf-blindness. My team contained other a total of 6 innovators from 4 countries: Egypt, Germany, India, and Indonesia, and all of the collaboration was handled via Zoom Video Software, presentation via Canva, Coding via Visual Studio Code.
